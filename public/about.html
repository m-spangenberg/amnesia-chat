AMNESIA CHAT

AMNESIA is a forgetful chat room with no usernames, no profiles, no logins and no long term memory. Please use your better judgement when speaking to strangers on the internet and never give out personal information. AMNESIA is currently still being heavily tinkered with, so things might break as improvements are made. Have fun!

AKNOWLEDGEMENTS

AMNESIA was built during the 2020 Covid-19 Lockdown as a WebSocket learning exercise. The code is available on GitHub under the MIT licence. The front-end makes use of Tailwind CSS. The back-end relies on Node.js, Express and Socket.io.
